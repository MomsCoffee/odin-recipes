Ingredients

  • 1 bucket of sour cream
  • 200g of smoked salmon
  • a handful of cherry tomatoes
  • 1 bucket of mascarpone
  • pasta of choice
  • head of garlic>

Steps

  1. Chop all the cloves of garlic
  2. Cut into the skins of tomatoes, in order to remove them later
  3. Pour boiling water over the tomatoes and leave until skins can be removed easily
  4. Fry the garlic in olive oil until it starts to turn golden
  5. Add the cream and mascarpone to the pan
  6. Slice the salmon into smaller pieces and add to pan
  7. Add tomatoes to pan, removing skins in the process
  8. Let it all become friendly for around 10 minutes, boiling the pasta in salted water in the meantime
  9. Strain the pasta and mix with the sauce
